Welcome to Reno/Tahoe Mountain Biking Adventures. The area surrounding Reno
Nevada and Lake Tahoe provides some of the worlds premier mountain biking. Ranging
from easy desert rides to challenging mountain climbs, the area encompasses rides for
every level of user. Additionally the area as a wide range of other amenities include both
bargain and five star lodging, great nightlife and other adventure activities.

This site describes some of the best mountain biking in the area including:

Trail Difficulty
The Famous Flume Trail Advanced
Keystone Canyon Beginner
Martlette Lake Intermediate
Steamboat Ditch Beginner
