Tahoe Mountain Biking Adventures

America’s Adventure Place

Welcome to Reno/Tahoe Mountain Biking Adventures. The area surrounding Reno Nevada and Lake Tahoe provides some of the world's premier mountain biking. Ranging from easy desert rides to challenging mountain climbs, the area encompasses rides for every level of user. Additionally the area as a wide range of other amenities includes both bargain and five star lodging, great nightlife and other adventure activities. This site describes some of the best mountain biking in the area including:
  • The Famous Flume Trail
  • Keystone Canyon
  • Martlette Lake
  • Steamboat Canal
Reno/Tahoe makes a perfect adventure vacation. For more details contact the Reno Tahoe Web site at www.visitrenotahoe.com Jonathan Smith
